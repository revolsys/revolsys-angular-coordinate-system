Distance and Azimuth

Given a pair of points, calculate the distance and azimuths (bearings) between the two points.

For geographic coordinate systems the Vincenty's inverse problem formula and coordinate system's ellipsoid is used. Latitude and Longitude coordinates, can be entered as either Degrees, Minutes, and Seconds (DMS) or Decimal degrees. DMS can be in any of the formats 9:9:9.999, 9Â°9'9.9", or 9 9 9.9.

For projected coordinate systems the euclidean distance and angle formula are used.
