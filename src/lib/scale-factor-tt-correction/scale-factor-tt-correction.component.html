Meridian Convergence & Point Scale Factor Computation

This utility calculates meridian convergence and point scale factor for a latitude/longitude point. The computations are a function of the defined NAD83 UTM coordinate system. The output is the meridian convergence (degrees-minutes-seconds) and the point scale factor. Meridian convergence is the angle between the tangent to a projected meridian of longitude on the mapping plane and grid north. The point scale factor describes, at the given point on the map projection, the amount of distortion in length with respect to the origin and is output as a ratio.
